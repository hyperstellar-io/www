What are the perks of working with HyperStellar Technology?

At HyperStellar Technology, our staff members value cooperation and concur that synergy can be powerful. We take ownership of every assignment to ensure effectiveness and transparent growth opportunities for everyone. We have a result-driven mindset and are fervently committed to producing great results. We place a high priority on self-education and training for ongoing development. We deliver projects on schedule and within budget, provide expert software audits, and provide continuous software support and maintenance after release.

What industries do you operate in?

Our outsourcing services for software development are specifically designed to meet the differing requirements of various sectors. Our primary area of concentration is product creation for important industries like telecoms, financial, real estate, healthcare, and e-commerce. But we have also been successful in completing projects for businesses in the entertainment, hospitality, media, and other sectors. We can offer the greatest services on the market because to our knowledge in numerous industries.

What software development services do you provide?

We offer a wide range of software development and design services that may be used either singly or across the entire project development life cycle. We also provide project management, product management, branding and UI/UX design, web development, mobile app development, QA services, MVP strategy consulting, AR/VR, blockchain, DevOps, and AR/VR. We have the knowledge to satisfy your objectives successfully, whether you need help with particular areas or end-to-end solutions.

Do you provide post-development support?

Yes, we provide post-development support and maintenance services also depending on your needs, we can offer technical support on an ongoing basis as well as bug fixes, feature upgrades, and security updates.

What does your software development process look like?

The software development life cycle (SDLC), which contains seven crucial phases, is how we develop our products. We start by analyzing customer requirements, gathering market data, and charting the roadmap. Concept design, code development, and the creation of a minimally viable product are the next steps. We effectively produce high-quality software through the use of our thoughtfully developed SDLC process, which also ensures balance and the best possible solutions throughout the development process.

How do we assure privacy and confidentiality?

Whenever the client requests it, we sign confidentiality and nondisclosure agreements. All of our staff members are employed full-time by us and are subject to company confidentiality and non-disclosure agreements.Additionally, we can sign unique NDAs for delicate tasks.

How do you guarantee product quality?

We review the outcome every two weeks, test our work (we carry out both development and acceptance tests), give it to you, and solicit comments from the client so they can be sure they are getting what they paid for. Additionally, we keep in constant contact with the client to avoid any surprises, and we only continue the development with their agreement.
